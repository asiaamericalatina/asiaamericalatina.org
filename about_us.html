About us

Aims and Scope of our Journal

Asia/AméricaLatina is a scientific (peer reviewed) semiannual academic publication that aims for the interaction between the two regions through the diffusion of articles, opinions and references of mutual interest. Defined as the product of bi-regional social studies, the journal accommodates a broad range of approaches regarding the past and present of both cultural areas, contact points and influences, as well as their specificities and communication and collaboration prospects. Asia/AméricaLatina falls in the framework of current international studies and broad academic cooperation.

Asia/AméricaLatina welcomes four types of articles: original researches, essays, news and book reviews. Pieces for publication may be submitted in Spanish, Portuguese or English.

The Asia/AméricaLatina Journal is supported by the University of Buenos Aires through its UBACyT grants and by the National Scientific and Technological Promotion Agency through its Scientific and Tehnological Research Projects (PICT).
